DSysphile

DSysphile is the grown out of Distributed System News Letter (DSNL) which started on 26th April 2018 as a hobby community. The community has grown fantastically with interesting and enthusiastic readers. Recently many of our readers asked for help to share their ideas in the field. So we created an interactive community DSysphile. This community is focused on discussions, ideas, knowledge sharing on distributed systems. We are starting a series of interactive videos with community members.
